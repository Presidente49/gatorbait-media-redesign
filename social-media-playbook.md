## Platform-Specific Tips

### YouTube Shorts (< 60 seconds)
- Vertical video (9:16)
- Hook in first 3 seconds
- Add captions (Restream includes transcripts)
- End with "Subscribe for daily Gator coverage"

### TikTok (< 3 minutes)
- Trending sounds boost reach (but talk clips do fine in sports)
- Reply to comments with video responses
- Post 1-3x daily for algorithm favor

### X / Twitter
- Quote-tweet your own clips with hot takes
- Engage with @GatorsOnline, @Gabordi, @SEC accounts
- Post during games for maximum engagement

### Facebook Reels
- Restream auto-posts here
- Older demographic = more engagement on longer analysis clips
- Share to Florida Gators fan groups

### Instagram Reels
- Download from Restream → upload manually (or use Repurpose.io)
- Use location tag: Gainesville, Florida
- Stories: reshare your Reels + add polls/questions
