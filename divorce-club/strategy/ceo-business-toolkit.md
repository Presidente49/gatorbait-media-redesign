## Executive Summary

This report maps every tool you need to run a CEO-level online business — from community management to email marketing to workflow automation. It combines two sources: **battle-tested open-source GitHub projects** (the "Gems") and **Manus Agent Skills** (AI-powered capabilities you can activate right now). ## Part 1: GitHub Gems — Open-Source Business Infrastructure

These are the highest-rated, most actively maintained open-source tools for each core business function. Every tool below is self-hostable, meaning you own your data and pay zero platform fees.

### Community Platform

| Tool | Stars | Tier | GitHub |
|------|-------|------|--------|
| **Discourse** | 46,941 | Excellent | [discourse/discourse](https://github.com/discourse/discourse) |

Discourse is the gold standard for online community forums. Used by companies like Samsung, Figma, and Docker, it provides threaded discussions, real-time notifications, trust levels, badges, and plugin architecture. For The Divorce Club, Discourse could power the private member community — a space for support, discussion, event coordination, and resource sharing. It integrates with SSO providers so members log in once and access everything. The trust-level system naturally surfaces quality contributors and moderates toxic behavior without heavy admin overhead.

### Online Course / LMS Platform

| Tool | Stars | Tier | GitHub |
|------|-------|------|--------|
| **Open edX** | 8,086 | Solid | [openedx/openedx-platform](https://github.com/openedx/openedx-platform) |

Open edX is the platform behind Harvard, MIT, and hundreds of universities. It provides a full learning management system with course authoring (Studio), student-facing LMS, certificates, grading, and discussion forums built in. For the Divorce Club ecosystem, Open edX could host paid courses: "Rebuilding Your Brand After Divorce," "Financial Independence Masterclass," or "Starting Over: The Playbook." It supports both free and paid enrollment, making it perfect for a tiered membership model.

### CRM / Contact Management

| Tool | Stars | Tier | GitHub |
|------|-------|------|--------|
| **Twenty** | 45,394 | Legendary | [twentyhq/twenty](https://github.com/twentyhq/twenty) |

Twenty is the open-source alternative to Salesforce, designed for the AI era. It provides contact management, deal tracking, pipeline visualization, email integration, and an extensible data model. For a CEO managing multiple business lines (Divorce Club, Marketing Mama, events, podcast), Twenty can track every lead, every deal, and every relationship in one place. Its AI features help automate follow-ups and surface insights about which leads are most likely to convert.

### Email Marketing / Newsletter

| Tool | Stars | Tier | GitHub |
|------|-------|------|--------|
| **Listmonk** | 19,874 | Excellent | [knadh/listmonk](https://github.com/knadh/listmonk) |

Listmonk is a high-performance, self-hosted newsletter and mailing list manager that ships as a single binary. It handles subscriber management, segmentation, campaign creation, analytics, and bounce handling. For the Divorce Club, Listmonk replaces Mailchimp or ConvertKit at zero cost. You can run drip sequences for new members, blast event announcements, promote podcast episodes, and segment your list by interest (events, podcast, book, community) — all without per-subscriber pricing.

### Podcast Hosting / Distribution

| Tool | Stars | Tier | GitHub |
|------|-------|------|--------|
| **Audiobookshelf** | 12,627 | Excellent | [advplyr/audiobookshelf](https://github.com/advplyr/audiobookshelf) |

Audiobookshelf is a self-hosted server for managing audiobooks and podcasts with a modern interface. It provides library organization, streaming to multiple devices, and listener analytics. For the "Uncoupled & Unstoppable" podcast, Audiobookshelf gives you full ownership of your content and listener data — no platform taking a cut or controlling your distribution. Pair it with a standard RSS feed and you can still distribute to Spotify, Apple Podcasts, and everywhere else while keeping the master copy on your own infrastructure.

### Event Management / Ticketing

| Tool | Stars | Tier | GitHub |
|------|-------|------|--------|
| **Attendize** | 4,248 | Solid | [Attendize/Attendize](https://github.com/Attendize/Attendize) |

Attendize is an open-source ticket selling and event management platform. It handles event creation, ticket sales, attendee management, check-in, and reporting — without paying Eventbrite's service fees. For the Divorce Club's in-person events (mixers, panels, comedy nights, workshops), Attendize lets you sell tickets directly from your own website, collect attendee data into your CRM, and keep 100% of ticket revenue.

### Landing Page / Funnel Builder

| Tool | Stars | Tier | GitHub |
|------|-------|------|--------|
| **Mautic** | 9,534 | Solid | [mautic/mautic](https://github.com/mautic/mautic) |

Mautic is an open-source marketing automation platform that combines landing page creation, email campaigns, lead scoring, and funnel tracking. It replaces ClickFunnels, HubSpot, and ActiveCampaign in a single self-hosted package. For the Divorce Club, Mautic can build high-converting opt-in pages, run automated welcome sequences, score leads based on engagement, and trigger the MarketingMama bridge workflow when someone shows interest in business services.

### Social Media Management

| Tool | Stars | Tier | GitHub |
|------|-------|------|--------|
| **Postiz** | 29,869 | Excellent | [gitroomhq/postiz-app](https://github.com/gitroomhq/postiz-app) |

Postiz is the open-source alternative to Buffer and Hootsuite. It provides multi-platform scheduling, analytics, team collaboration, and AI-assisted content creation. For managing The Divorce Club's Instagram, Facebook, TikTok, and LinkedIn presence, Postiz lets you schedule weeks of content in advance, track what's performing, and collaborate with a social media manager — all without monthly SaaS fees.

### Business Automation / Workflow

| Tool | Stars | Tier | GitHub |
|------|-------|------|--------|
| **n8n** | 186,406 | Legendary | [n8n-io/n8n](https://github.com/n8n-io/n8n) |

n8n is the most popular open-source workflow automation platform, connecting 400+ applications with a visual drag-and-drop builder. It replaces Zapier, Make.com, and custom integration code. For the Divorce Club ecosystem, n8n is the glue that connects everything: new email signup triggers a welcome sequence in Listmonk, event RSVP creates a contact in Twenty, podcast episode publish triggers social media posts in Postiz, and a high-engagement lead gets flagged for the MarketingMama pipeline.

### Analytics / Business Intelligence

| Tool | Stars | Tier | GitHub |
|------|-------|------|--------|
| **Metabase** | 47,140 | Excellent | [metabase/metabase](https://github.com/metabase/metabase) |

Metabase is a user-friendly business intelligence tool that connects to any database and creates interactive dashboards without writing SQL. For a CEO running multiple business lines, Metabase provides a single dashboard showing: website traffic, email subscriber growth, event ticket sales, podcast downloads, community engagement, and revenue — all in real-time. It turns raw data into decisions.
