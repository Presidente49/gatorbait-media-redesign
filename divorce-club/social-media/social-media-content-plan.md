## Platform Strategy

| Platform | Purpose | Content Type | Frequency |
|----------|---------|-------------|-----------|
| Instagram | Primary brand hub | Posts, Stories, Reels | 3-4x/week |
| TikTok | Viral reach & humor | Short-form video, humor | 3-5x/week |
| Facebook | Community group | Group discussions, events | Daily in group |
| LinkedIn | Professional credibility | Articles, event promos | 1-2x/week |
| YouTube | Podcast video + long-form | Full podcast episodes | Weekly |
| Email | Direct relationship | Newsletter, event invites | Weekly |

## Tone & Voice Guidelines
- **DO:** Be funny, irreverent, empowering, real, vulnerable, cheeky
- **DON'T:** Be bitter, mean-spirited, preachy, or overly serious
- **Think:** "Your best friend who also happens to be a therapist and a bartender"
- **Reference points:** Fight Club vibes, nightclub energy, real talk
